South East Asian Institute of Technology

South East Asian Institute of Technology, Inc. is a recognized Higher Education Institution in Tupi, South Cotabato, known for its commitment to providing accessible and affordable education to Indigenous communities and neighboring tribes.

Mission, Vision & Core Values

Mission

To produce competent community-oriented and globally competitive individuals through holistic education.

Vision

A premier institution that provides quality education and globally empowered individuals.

Core Values

  • Service
  • Excellence
  • Accountability
  • Innovation
  • Teamwork
